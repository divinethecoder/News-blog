Reactions as BBNaija’s Nini prefers being rich, sad

Big Brother Naija reality show star, Anita Singh, has stirred reactions on social media as she drops her take on being rich or poor, no matter the circumstances surrounding it. The BBNaija season 6 housemate said she prefers to be rich and sad than to be happy and poor. Nini in a post on her Instagram page, shared a video where she was asked to choose between ”happy but poor and rich but sad”. She shocked many of her fans when she said she ”would choose flying all over the world in her private jets, offending with Fendi, crying inside her palatial house or luxurious whip over being poor so that she can maintain her baby girl for life.



“Baby girl status for life, who else wants to cry in Lambo with me?” Reactions: Denrele wrote; “This generation is gone. Why can’t you be average and be happy instead?
